About the Author

Hi. My name is Kailah Hinckson, and I am a teenaged published author. Here is a bit about me. When I actually started writing books, like the ones that people actually like to read, I would do it all the time. My mom would notice me, and then one day said this to me, "Kailah! When you were younger, as soon as you learned to write, you would make books." It is true. After I learned how to write in kintergarten, I used to come home and staple printer-paper together--to write my own stories in. This continued until third grade, when our family moved to Texas. But then, approxiamtely five years later, I started writing again. No, not little picture books about "Kitty Queen," but actual novels, like *The Seven Swords* which is coming soon. I am so happy to share my writing journey with you, and I hope you enjoy my writings.

Readers don’t just follow stories. They follow storytellers.
